Mosaic of Movements: ARTistic History Visualized

Custom Visualisation

This visualization transforms individual artworks into a mosaic forming the letters ART. Artworks are grouped by art movement (see legend for colors).

Give it sometime to load all the images

Design Details

Marks: Artwork images arranged to form the word "ART" with chronological organization from left to right.

Channels:

  • Color overlay - Represents art movement (different colors for different movements)
  • Gold dot - Indicates especially high-priced artworks

Interactions:

  • Hover - Shows detailed tooltip with title, artist, year, price, and movement
  • Hover over an artist - Highlights all artworks by the same artist
  • Click - Highlights all artworks by the same artist, connecting them with yellow lines and keeping the highlight in place
    • Click outside to remove highlighting
  • Double-click - Opens full-screen browsing mode to explore all artworks by that artist

Custom Visualization Category

This visualization falls into the novel category because it creates a unique representation by combining:

  1. Text-based layout generation using canvas drawing
  2. Chronological data arrangement within a typographic structure
  3. Artist relationship highlighting with interactive connection lines
Rather than modifying an existing chart type, this visualization was built from scratch to create a new way of exploring art collections that reveals temporal, stylistic, and artist connections simultaneously.
